Information About Girls Who Code.

  • Girls Who Code has gone from 20 girls in New York to 40,000 girls in 50 states. That is the same number of girls who graduate each year with a degree in computer science. That is progress! I am proud to say we are not just aiming to close the gender gap in tech: we are actually doing it. When girls learn to code, they become change agents in their communities. Whether it is a game to illustrate the experience of an undocumented immigrant or a website to provide free college prep, our girls create technology that makes the world a better place. Like us, you believed in girls’ unlimited potential. Thanks to your support and contributions, together we have inspired thousands of girls to see a future in tech.
